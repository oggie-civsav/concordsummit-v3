Who We Are

A National Capital Markets Platform

Concord Summit Capital is a national commercial real estate capital markets advisory firm and one of the leading intermediaries between sponsors and providers of capital for commercial real estate debt and equity financing.

Our advisory team provides flexible financing solutions for complex transactions across all major asset classes: multifamily, office, industrial, retail, hospitality, and mixed-use.

Our investment team targets illiquid and distressed assets and non-performing loans to extract excess return premiums.

We operate across five major markets: Miami (headquarters), New York, Dallas–Fort Worth, Denver, and Los Angeles.
